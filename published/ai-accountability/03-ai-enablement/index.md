# AI Enablement

AI enablement is the discipline of getting an organization to actually adopt AI — to change how people work, not just what tools they can open. This bundle is the operator's manual for the person accountable for that: the **AI Enablement Lead**.

It is the practitioner counterpart to [Enterprise AI Transformation](/enterprise-ai-transformation/index.md), which covers the whole program at the leadership altitude. Here we go deep on the people engine — the part the evidence says most often decides whether AI delivers.

New here? Start with [What AI Enablement Is — and the Role](/ai-accountability/03-ai-enablement/what-ai-enablement-is.md).
