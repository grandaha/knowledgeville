# AI Risk Officer

A model can be perfectly legal, perfectly on-strategy, and still wrong. That's what the **AI Risk Officer** exists to catch — model-level risk: accuracy, validation, and drift, rooted in bank model-risk-management practice and pointed at AI/ML models. This bundle is the operator's manual for that seat.

It's one entry in [AI Accountability](/ai-accountability/index.md)'s role-by-role map — distinct from [AI Enablement](/ai-accountability/03-ai-enablement/index.md) (adoption), [Chief AI Officer](/ai-accountability/01-chief-ai-officer/index.md) (strategy), and [AI Transformation Lead](/ai-accountability/02-ai-transformation-lead/index.md) (program execution): this seat owns whether the models themselves can be trusted.

New here? Start with [What the AI Risk Officer Is — and the Role](/ai-accountability/05-ai-risk-officer/what-ai-risk-officer-is.md).
