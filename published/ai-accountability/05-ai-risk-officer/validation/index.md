# Validation

This bundle uses a **selective** citation posture. The claims that carry weight — the regulatory definitions, scope, and the explicit generative/agentic AI exclusion — are cited verbatim to OCC/Federal Reserve/FDIC Bulletin 2026-13 and verified. Practitioner framing (how this role differs from AI Governance Lead and a Chief Risk Officer) is original synthesis, presented as such.

Every embedded fact is verified against a primary source before it ships (**verify-embedded-facts**), whether or not it carries a citation. This bundle's `temporal_mode` is **point-in-time-supersedable**: Bulletin 2026-13 itself notes a request for information on generative/agentic AI is forthcoming, so this content is understood to be current as of the bulletin's issuance, not a permanent statement of scope. Verified claims live in `evidence.yaml`; each page's `## Sources` list is generated from it. Validation sweeps are recorded here as dated audits.
