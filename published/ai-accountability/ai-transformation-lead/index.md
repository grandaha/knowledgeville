# AI Transformation Lead

A strategy is a direction. Turning it into a running, funded, prioritized portfolio of work is a different job. That's the **AI Transformation Lead** — the person who runs the cross-functional AI program day to day, translating a Chief AI Officer's strategy into delivery. This bundle is the operator's manual for that seat.

It's one entry in [AI Accountability](/ai-accountability/index.md)'s role-by-role map — the practitioner counterpart to [Chief AI Officer](/ai-accountability/chief-ai-officer/index.md) (which sets the direction this role executes) and [AI Enablement](/ai-accountability/ai-enablement/index.md) (which owns one workstream, adoption, inside the portfolio this role runs).

New here? Start with [What the AI Transformation Lead Is — and the Role](/ai-accountability/ai-transformation-lead/what-ai-transformation-lead-is.md).
