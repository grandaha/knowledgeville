# Does AI Coding Actually Help?

The honest answer is: it depends, and the variables that determine "on what" are not the
ones most adoption pitches mention. This section starts with the evidence itself —
including where a controlled study directly contradicts vendor-reported speedups — then
works through what actually predicts whether AI coding assistance helps a given developer
on a given task, and ends with how to measure it for your own team instead of trusting
someone else's number.
