# Validation

How this bundle is validated — sourced claims are checked, and each validation sweep is recorded here as a dated audit.

This bundle is `citation_style: deeply-cited` (see `research-profile.yaml`): the evidence
base — a controlled productivity study, named CVEs, documented attack campaigns,
peer-reviewed security research — is held to the same standard as its primary sources, not
summarized secondhand. No claim ships until it traces to a fetched primary source in
`evidence.yaml`. Audits of specific pages, once authored, are recorded in `audits/` here.
