# Strategy and Investment

Where is the money in AI actually going to end up? This theme covers the strategic and
investment side of the industry — the bets analysts and investors are making about which
layer of the AI stack captures the value as the technology matures.

The briefs here take an analyst's investment thesis and lay out the argument in plain
language: what they claim, the evidence they lean on, and — because a thesis often comes
from someone with money riding on it — where their stake shapes the story. You get the
reasoning and the honest read, so you can weigh the call for yourself.
