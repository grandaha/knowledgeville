# Workforce and Skills

This theme covers what AI is doing to work itself — which jobs are exposed, how roles are
changing, and what it means for the people doing them. The numbers here move fast and the
headlines run hot, so the briefs try to separate the real signal from the fear.

The briefs draw on analyst studies and workforce surveys. They report the figure, then show
you how it was defined and where a stake or a loose definition should make you read carefully.
The goal is to give you a clear-eyed view of the workforce shift, not a scare or a sales pitch.
