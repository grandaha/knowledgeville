# Market and Adoption

There's a gap between the AI headlines and what's actually happening inside enterprises.
This theme covers that gap — how fast organizations are really adopting AI, where
adoption stalls, what it costs, and what the numbers mean for buyers and vendors making
decisions right now.

The briefs here draw on analyst takes and market surveys. They don't just report a
headline number — they show you the argument behind it and flag where the evidence is
thin. The goal is to give you the context to judge what the market signals actually mean
for your organization.
