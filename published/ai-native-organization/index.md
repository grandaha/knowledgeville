# The AI-Native Organization

Enterprise AI Transformation and AI Accountability both describe the transition — the
journey, and who runs it. This bundle describes the destination: the shape an
organization actually takes once that transition is done. It is a short, argued thesis
(three pages, not a track-based roadmap), built from current research rather than
speculation, and meant to be weighed rather than executed.

See [The AI-Native Organization](/ai-native-organization/the-ai-native-organization.md)
for the full map of how the three pages relate.
