# Evaluation and Benchmarks

Every "our model beats the rest" claim rests on how it was measured. Papers here are about
the measuring itself — new benchmarks, better ways to test real-world performance, and the
recurring problem of models scoring well on a test while failing at the actual task.

If you read AI announcements, this is the theme that helps you read the scores critically. A
benchmark can be gamed, leaked into training data, or not measure what its name suggests.
The briefs here help you tell a meaningful result from an impressive-looking one.
