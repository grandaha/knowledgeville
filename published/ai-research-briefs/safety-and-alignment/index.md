# Safety and Alignment

Keeping AI useful, honest, and under control. Papers here cover alignment methods, guardrails,
red-teaming, jailbreaks, and measuring real-world harms — from confident wrong answers to
misuse.

Safety research ranges from rigorous to speculative, and it's not always obvious which is
which. The briefs in this theme try to stay concrete: what was tested, what broke, and what
a practitioner should do about it. If you're responsible for an AI system that real people touch, this is the
theme that flags what can go wrong and what's being done about it.
