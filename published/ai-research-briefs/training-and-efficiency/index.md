# Training and Efficiency

How models get built, tuned, and made cheaper to run. Papers here cover training methods,
fine-tuning, distillation, quantization, and the steady push to get the same quality for
less compute, memory, and money.

This theme matters even if you never train a model yourself, because efficiency is what
makes a capability affordable at your scale. The briefs focus on the practical question
behind the math: does this make good AI cheaper to run, and what do you give up?
