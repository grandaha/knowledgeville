# Validation

Before any brief is published, every figure in it — sample sizes, scores, dates, funding —
is checked against the paper itself. If we can't confirm a number against the source, we
drop it or flag it. We never pass along a statistic we couldn't verify.

This is the bundle's verification posture: **verify the embedded facts**. The single source
for a brief is the paper it covers, so we check every major claim directly against that
paper. We don't re-run the experiments — we confirm the brief faithfully reports what the
paper says.

Each validation sweep is recorded here as a dated audit, so the checking is visible and you
can see when a brief was last reviewed.
