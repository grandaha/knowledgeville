Plain-language definitions of the terms this bundle uses. It grows as the bundle does.

- **Playbook** — a procedure your AI assistant reads and runs on its own, start to finish, rather than a single prompt you paste.
- **Agent-executable** — written so an AI assistant can follow the steps directly, not just so a person can understand them.
- **Connector (tool)** — the link that lets your assistant read a service such as your email or calendar. Also called a tool or an integration.
- **Read-first** — the assistant reads and drafts, but does not send, delete, or change anything on its own.
- **Trigger** — when the playbook is meant to run: on a schedule (each morning) or when you ask.
- **Guardrails** — the limits the assistant must respect while running a playbook, such as never sending mail or acting on anything ambiguous.
