# Brainstorming & Ideation

Brainstorming alone has a ceiling — you keep circling the same ideas. An AI chat tool makes a useful thinking partner here. It generates options quickly and without judgment, which lets you move past the obvious and into territory you might not have reached on your own. Don't expect every suggestion to be good; most won't be. The value is volume and variety — you pick, combine, and discard until something sticks.

The two recipes here get you started: [Brainstorm Angles and Ideas](/ai-use-cases/everyday-tasks/brainstorming-ideation/brainstorm-angles-and-ideas.md) and [Generate Names and Titles](/ai-use-cases/everyday-tasks/brainstorming-ideation/generate-names-and-titles.md).
