# Editing & Rewriting

When you already have a draft, AI is at its most reliable. You're not asking it to invent — you're asking it to improve something real. It's good at catching awkward sentences, tightening wordy paragraphs, and shifting the register of a piece (casual to professional, direct to diplomatic). The important constraint: always paste in your actual text. Vague instructions like "make this better" don't work well without the source material.

The two recipes here cover the most common editing jobs: [Fix Grammar and Tighten](/ai-use-cases/everyday-tasks/editing-rewriting/fix-grammar-and-tighten.md) and [Change the Tone](/ai-use-cases/everyday-tasks/editing-rewriting/change-the-tone.md).
