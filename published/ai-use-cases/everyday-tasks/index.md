# Everyday Tasks

**Everyday Tasks** is a set of copy-paste recipes for getting real work done with an AI chat tool — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, whichever you have open. It's for people who aren't here to learn about AI in the abstract; you have an email to write, a document to summarize, a decision to think through, and you want to do it now.

No code. No accounts to connect. No jargon. If you can paste text into a chat box, you can use everything here.

## What's inside each page

Every page is a **recipe, not an essay**:

- a one-line sense of when to reach for it,
- a **ready-to-paste prompt** with obvious blanks to fill in, and
- a **before → after** so you can see the kind of result to expect.

Copy the prompt, swap in your own details, and run it. You should walk away having *done* the task.

The recipes are grouped by the **kind of task** — the verb — not by your job or by a specific app. Pick the one that matches what you're trying to do:

## One honest note

AI is great at first drafts, options, and structure — and it will sometimes state something wrong with total confidence. Treat what it gives you as a strong starting point, not gospel: skim it, fix what's off, and check any fact that matters before you rely on it. The [glossary](/ai-use-cases/everyday-tasks/glossary.md) explains the few terms used here.
