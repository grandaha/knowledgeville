# Learning & Practicing

AI chat tools are unusually good learning companions because they're endlessly patient and instantly interactive. You can ask for a quiz, get immediate feedback, ask follow-up questions, and repeat until something sticks — all at your own pace. They're also useful for practicing skills that involve conversation: job interviews, difficult talks, a language you're learning. The experience isn't perfect (the AI will sometimes be too easy on you), but for low-stakes practice with fast feedback, it's hard to beat.

The two recipes here show you how: [Quiz Me on a Topic](/ai-use-cases/everyday-tasks/learning-practicing/quiz-me-on-a-topic.md) and [Practice a Conversation (Roleplay)](/ai-use-cases/everyday-tasks/learning-practicing/practice-a-conversation-roleplay.md).
