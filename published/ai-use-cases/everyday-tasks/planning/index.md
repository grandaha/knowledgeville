# Planning

When you know what you want to accomplish but not how to sequence the steps, AI can help you build a structure fast. Tell it your goal, your constraints (time, budget, who's involved), and ask for a plan or checklist. It's good at covering the bases and thinking through logical order. You'll almost always need to adjust the output — AI doesn't know your specific situation the way you do — but having a draft plan to react to is much faster than building one from nothing.

The two recipes here show you how: [Make a Plan or Checklist](/ai-use-cases/everyday-tasks/planning/make-a-plan-or-checklist.md) and [Build a Schedule or Itinerary](/ai-use-cases/everyday-tasks/planning/build-a-schedule-or-itinerary.md).
