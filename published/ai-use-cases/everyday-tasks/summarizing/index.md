# Summarizing

Reading a long document to find the three things that matter is tedious work. AI handles it well — paste in the text and ask for a summary at whatever level of detail you need. One caution: AI can occasionally drop something important or smooth over a nuance, so if the document is high-stakes, skim the original too. But for everyday reading loads — long emails, reports, articles — summarizing is one of the most time-saving things you can do with an AI chat tool.

The two recipes here show you how: [Summarize a Document](/ai-use-cases/everyday-tasks/summarizing/summarize-a-document.md) and [Pull Out Action Items](/ai-use-cases/everyday-tasks/summarizing/pull-out-action-items.md).
