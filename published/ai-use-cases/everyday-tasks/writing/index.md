# Writing

Starting from scratch is often the hardest part of writing. AI chat tools are genuinely useful here — not because they write for you, but because they can break the blank-page paralysis fast. You give them context (who you're writing to, what you want to say, the tone you're after), and they hand back a working draft you can shape into your own. The output usually needs editing, but editing is easier than starting cold.

The two recipes in this section cover the two most common from-scratch tasks: [Draft an Email](/ai-use-cases/everyday-tasks/writing/draft-an-email.md) and [Social Posts and Captions](/ai-use-cases/everyday-tasks/writing/social-posts-and-captions.md).
