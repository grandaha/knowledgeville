# Board & Investor

A board reads carefully and asks hard questions, so the work here is about clarity and anticipation. AI can shape the quarter's facts into a clear, honest update and help you war-game the questions a board or investor will press on — so you walk in ready rather than surprised. Keep the discipline: every figure in an update is checked against the source, and the answers you rehearse are your real positions, not whatever sounds smooth.
