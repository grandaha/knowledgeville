# Communications

A leader's words carry further than anyone else's, so tone is the whole game. AI is a strong drafting partner for the all-hands note or company-wide message, and a careful sounding board for the sensitive or crisis message where one wrong line does real damage. The high-stakes ones are workflows, not one-shot prompts, on purpose: you want to slow down, weigh how it lands, and sign off on every word yourself.
