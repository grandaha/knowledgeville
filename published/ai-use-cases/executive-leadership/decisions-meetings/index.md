# Decisions & Meetings

Most leadership time goes to decisions and the meetings around them, and both get better when they're made explicit. AI can turn a pending call into a crisp decision memo others can act on, build an agenda and pre-read that drive a meeting toward decisions instead of updates, and help you reason through competing priorities to a defensible ranking. The thinking is still yours — these tools force the trade-offs into the open so you can make the call, not make it for you.

- **[Write a Decision Memo](/ai-use-cases/executive-leadership/decisions-meetings/write-a-decision-memo.md)** — turn a pending call into something others can act on
- **[Prep and Run a Leadership Meeting](/ai-use-cases/executive-leadership/decisions-meetings/prep-and-run-a-leadership-meeting.md)** — build an agenda and pre-read that drive to decisions
- **[Prioritize Across Competing Demands](/ai-use-cases/executive-leadership/decisions-meetings/prioritize-across-competing-demands.md)** — work to a defensible ranking
