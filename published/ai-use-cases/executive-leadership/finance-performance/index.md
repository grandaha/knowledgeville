# Finance & Performance

You don't need AI to do the math — your finance team and your tools already do. What it's good at is helping you *interrogate* the numbers: sharper questions to put to a report, the trade-offs hiding in a budget, a dense deck boiled down to the one page you can act on. The rule that matters here: AI can misread or miscopy a figure, so never let it restate a number you'll repeat without checking it against the source.

- **[Prep for a Budget Review](/ai-use-cases/executive-leadership/finance-performance/prep-for-a-budget-review.md)** — know the trade-offs going in
- **[Turn a Finance Deck into a One-Page Read](/ai-use-cases/executive-leadership/finance-performance/turn-a-finance-deck-into-a-one-page-read.md)** — compress a long deck into something you can act on
