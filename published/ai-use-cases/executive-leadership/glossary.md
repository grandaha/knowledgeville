Plain-language definitions of the terms this bundle uses. It grows as the bundle does.

**Prompt** — the text you paste into an AI chat tool to tell it what you want. Most recipes here hand you one to copy and fill in.

**Recipe** — a page built around a ready-to-paste prompt for one concrete task, with a before-and-after so you can see the result.

**Workflow** — a page that walks you through a judgment-heavy job step by step, using AI where it helps rather than handing you a single prompt.

**Pre-read** — a short document sent before a meeting so everyone arrives on the same page and the meeting can start on the real questions.

**Scenario** — one possible version of the future (best case, base case, worst case) used to stress-test a plan before you commit to it.
