# People & Org

People decisions are the ones you most want to get right and least want to rush. AI can lay out the options behind an org or hiring call, turn rough notes into clear and fair feedback, and let you rehearse a hard conversation before you have it for real. Two cautions matter more here than anywhere else: never paste in details that identify a specific employee to a tool you don't control, and remember the empathy and the decision are yours — AI drafts the words, not the relationship.
