# Strategy & Planning

Strategy work is where AI earns its keep as a thinking partner, not a ghostwriter. It can pull scattered inputs into a tight pre-read, give you a fast first read on a market or competitor, and play devil's advocate against a plan you're close to — but the bets and the trade-offs stay yours. The constraint: feed it your real situation, and treat what it gives you as a starting point for your own thinking, not a verdict.
