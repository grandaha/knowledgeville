# AI Use Cases

**AI Use Cases** is a growing library of practical, hands-on ways to put AI to work — the kind you can try the moment you finish reading. It's organized into **sub-domains**, each a focused collection for a particular kind of user or setting. More will be added over time.

Four sub-domains are live today. **[Everyday Tasks](/ai-use-cases/everyday-tasks/index.md)** is copy-paste recipes for everyday work, written for someone who just wants to get something done with an AI chat tool, with no technical setup. **[Executive Leadership](/ai-use-cases/executive-leadership/index.md)** is AI recipes and workflows for the C-suite — board updates, budget reviews, org calls, and the messages that can't land wrong. **[Realtors](/ai-use-cases/realtors/index.md)** is paste-ready recipes for real-estate agents — listings, prospecting, and client communication. **[AI Playbooks](/ai-use-cases/ai-playbooks/index.md)** is a step up: procedures an AI assistant reads and runs on its own across your email and calendar — a morning brief, inbox triage, meeting prep — reading and drafting, never sending or changing anything.

New here? Start with **[The Use-Case Map](/ai-use-cases/the-use-case-map.md)** to see how everything is laid out, then jump straight to whatever you're trying to do.
