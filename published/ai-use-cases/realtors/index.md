# Realtors

This bundle is a set of paste-ready AI recipes for real-estate agents. Each page hands you
a prompt you can drop into ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini, plus a before-and-after so you can
see what it produces. No theory, no jargon — just the work you already do, done faster.

It's for a working agent, not a marketer or a developer. If you write listings, chase leads,
and keep clients warm through a deal, these are for you. You bring the facts and the
judgment; the AI drafts the words.

The recipes are grouped by the job you're trying to do:

- **[Listings & Marketing](/ai-use-cases/realtors/listings-and-marketing/index.md)** — turn property facts into descriptions, posts, and open-house promo.
- **[Prospecting & Outreach](/ai-use-cases/realtors/prospecting-and-outreach/index.md)** — farming letters, expired and FSBO scripts, and referral asks.
- **[Client Communication](/ai-use-cases/realtors/client-communication/index.md)** — follow-ups, plain-language explainers, and review requests.

One rule runs through all of it: **the AI drafts, you own the result.** Fair-housing law,
MLS rules, and the plain accuracy of what you claim are yours to check every time. Where a
recipe touches a rule you can't get wrong, it says so. Start with
[How AI Helps Realtors](/ai-use-cases/realtors/how-ai-helps-realtors.md) for the map.
