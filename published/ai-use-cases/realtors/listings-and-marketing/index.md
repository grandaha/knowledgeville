# Listings & Marketing

The copy that sells a property. Give the AI the facts — beds, baths, the one feature buyers
will love — and it drafts the listing, the social posts, and the open-house promo. You edit
for voice and check every line for fair-housing language before it goes live.

The recipes here:

- **[Write a Listing Description](/ai-use-cases/realtors/listings-and-marketing/write-a-listing-description.md)** — property facts into a description that sells the home, not the buyer.
- **[Just-Listed and Just-Sold Posts](/ai-use-cases/realtors/listings-and-marketing/just-listed-and-just-sold-posts.md)** — one listing or sale into platform-ready social posts.
- **[Open-House Promo Kit](/ai-use-cases/realtors/listings-and-marketing/open-house-promo-kit.md)** — flyer, email, and social copy for an open house.
