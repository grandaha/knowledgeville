# Prospecting & Outreach

The messages that start conversations. These recipes draft the outreach you keep meaning to
send — the neighborhood letter, the note to an expired listing, the ask to your own sphere —
so a cold task becomes a quick edit. You set the tone and mind the contact rules.

The recipes here:

- **[Neighborhood Farming Letter](/ai-use-cases/realtors/prospecting-and-outreach/neighborhood-farming-letter.md)** — a farming letter or postcard for a target area.
- **[Expired and FSBO Scripts](/ai-use-cases/realtors/prospecting-and-outreach/expired-and-fsbo-scripts.md)** — outreach for expired listings and for-sale-by-owner sellers.
- **[Sphere and Referral Asks](/ai-use-cases/realtors/prospecting-and-outreach/sphere-and-referral-asks.md)** — referral requests to your sphere that don't feel pushy.
