# Decision Guides

Some decisions come up again and again, they matter, and getting them wrong is expensive. This
bundle is a growing set of guides for exactly those calls — written to help you decide well, not
to decide for you.

Each guide lays out the real options, the criteria that actually matter, the tradeoffs with the
evidence behind them, and when each choice tends to win. The framing is our reasoning; the facts
behind the tradeoffs are cited so you can check them. Ask a generic chatbot to help you decide and
you get a confident, unsourced list. A guide here gives your AI assistant something better to work
from: grounded criteria and sourced tradeoffs it can apply to your situation.

Start with [How These Guides Work](/decision-guides/how-these-guides-work.md) for the shape they
all share, or go straight to [Build vs. Buy](/decision-guides/build-vs-buy.md).
