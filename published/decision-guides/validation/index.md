# Validation

How this bundle is validated. These guides carry a `selective` citation posture: the *framing* —
which options exist, which criteria matter, when each choice wins — is our reasoning and stands on
its own, while the *facts and tradeoff claims* that a decision turns on are cited to their sources.

Under `verify-embedded-facts`, every concrete factual claim (a statistic, an outcome, an
attribution) is checked against a primary source before it ships — and, being cited, it also
carries that source on the page. A guide is `point-in-time-supersedable`: it reflects the evidence
and the landscape as of its date, and a later guide supersedes it rather than editing history.
Each validation sweep is recorded here as a dated audit.
