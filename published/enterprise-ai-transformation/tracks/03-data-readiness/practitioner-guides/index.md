# Practitioner Guides

* [Practitioner Guides](overview.md) - Operational how-to guides for each AI data readiness discipline — methodology, tooling, and step-by-step implementation.
* [Data Audits & Automated Quality Governance](data-audits-and-automated-quality-governance.md) - A practitioner guide to conducting data audits and automating continuous quality governance so data quality becomes an operational property of the infrastructure.
* [Data Contracts](data-contracts.md) - A practitioner guide to data contracts — formal, enforced agreements between data producers and consumers that prevent silent breaking changes, expressed as code.
* [Data Labeling & Annotation Programs](data-labeling-and-annotation-programs.md) - A practitioner guide to running production-grade data labeling and annotation programs, where label quality determines model quality.
* [Data Mesh Governance in Practice](data-mesh-governance-in-practice.md) - A practitioner guide to data mesh — redistributing data ownership and accountability to domain teams, and enforcing enterprise standards through federated computational governance.
* [Historical Data Debt — Pre-AI vs. Post-AI Remediation](historical-data-debt-pre-ai-vs-post-ai-remediation.md) - A practitioner guide to assessing and remediating the years of historical data you already have before — or after — it trains an AI model.
* [Master Data Management (MDM)](master-data-management.md) - A practitioner guide to creating and maintaining a single trusted authoritative version of core business entities, and why MDM is foundational to AI quality.
* [Synthetic Data Generation](synthetic-data-generation.md) - A practitioner guide to generating synthetic data that statistically mirrors real data — when to use it, how it is generated, and the non-negotiable role of fidelity validation.
