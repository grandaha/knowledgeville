The eight workstreams of enterprise AI transformation. Each track contains a core framework, practitioner guides, and assessment tools.

| Track | The core question |
| --- | --- |
| AI Strategy & Leadership | What are we trying to accomplish with AI, and how does it connect to how we compete? |
| AI Governance & Risk | How do we deploy AI responsibly, safely, and in compliance with regulation? |
| Data Readiness | Is the data that AI depends on accurate, accessible, governed, and fit for purpose? |
| Technology Architecture & Platform | Do we have a coherent AI platform, or a collection of disconnected point solutions? |
| Workflow Optimization & Automation | Which workflows should be redesigned with AI, and how do we do it? |
| AI Adoption & Culture | Are people actually using AI, and are they thinking differently about their work? |
| Talent & Capability Building | Do we have — or are we building — the competencies AI transformation requires? |
| Measurement & Value Realization | Can we prove AI is working, and are we using that signal to improve? |
