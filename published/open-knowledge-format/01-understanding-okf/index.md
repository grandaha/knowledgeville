# Understanding OKF

This arc covers OKF as it stands today — the format, not the future. It answers the three
questions a leader tends to ask first: what is the actual model, where does it fit against tools I
already use, and can I trust what it tells me?

[How OKF Works](/open-knowledge-format/01-understanding-okf/01-how-it-works.md) lays out the model
at a practical altitude. [Where OKF Fits](/open-knowledge-format/01-understanding-okf/02-where-it-fits.md)
places it against RAG, knowledge graphs, and plain docs. [Provenance and Trust](/open-knowledge-format/01-understanding-okf/03-provenance-and-trust.md)
covers the part that matters most when an agent is the reader: knowing where a claim came from.
