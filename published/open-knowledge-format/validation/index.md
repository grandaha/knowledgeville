# Validation

How this bundle is validated. It carries a `selective` citation posture: factual claims about
what OKF is are cited to the canonical specification and verified against it, while the
forward-looking analysis in *The Potential* is presented as our view, not as sourced fact.

Under `verify-embedded-facts`, every concrete claim about the format is checked against the
canonical OKF specification before it ships — a quality gate, whether or not the claim carries a
visible citation. Verified, sourced claims live in `evidence.yaml`; each validation sweep is
recorded here as a dated audit.
