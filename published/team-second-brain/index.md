# Team Second Brain

Everyone on a small team already uses AI tools. None of what any one person learns from
using them reaches anyone else on the team. This bundle is for a team lead or founder at a
small business — anywhere from two people to fifteen — who wants that knowledge to compound
across the team instead of staying locked in each person's own account.

It builds on a real, credited foundation rather than inventing a new one: Andrej Karpathy's
LLM Wiki pattern, where an AI tool maintains a living markdown wiki instead of starting over
each session. [The Team Second Brain](/team-second-brain/the-team-second-brain.md) names
that pattern and the specific gap in it — every real implementation so far is built for one
person. [The Single-Player Ceiling](/team-second-brain/01-the-single-player-ceiling.md) surveys
what's actually out there. [Building Yours Without an Engineer](/team-second-brain/02-building-yours-without-an-engineer.md)
covers how to close that gap without hiring anyone or buying an enterprise platform.
