# Validation

How this bundle is validated — sourced claims are checked, and each validation sweep is
recorded here as a dated audit.

This bundle cites selectively: the specific claims that carry real weight — Karpathy's own
words describing the LLM Wiki pattern, and the facts about what other projects actually do —
are traced to their primary source and recorded in `evidence.yaml`. Connective explanation
is not forced into that same ceremony. Because a repository's star count, activity, and
pricing page all drift, every sourced claim here is point-in-time-supersedable: verified as
of a specific date, expected to need a fresh check rather than standing forever.
